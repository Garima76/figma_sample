The Frame
My name is Jonathan Maples. I trained in Japan with the Shimizu family in the art form called Hyousou. It deals with fabric, paper, glue, and wood to create these picture frames.
Traditional and Mordern Techniques
Your tapestry photo frames are made with both handmade technique and the assistance of machinery. Each one is custom made, so there are slight variancesin frame length and width. It takes 5 business days to make your order and ship.
